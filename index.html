About Emacity

Founded after more than 200 MUN experiences, Emacity Model United Nations was created with a simple belief: every delegate deserves an equal opportunity to learn, lead, and be heard. What began as an idea evolved into a platform dedicated to fairness, accessibility, confidence-building, and diplomacy.

🎖️ Founder Story

Nevin Mehta

After attending over 200 MUNs, founder Nevin Mehta envisioned a conference built by delegates for delegates. What started as a simple idea became a PNG logo, then a vision, and ultimately a conference hosting hundreds of delegates.
